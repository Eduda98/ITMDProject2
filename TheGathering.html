The Gathering

I'm the president of The Gathering on campus, a club dedicated to the Magic: The Gathering Trading Card Game.

Magic: The Gathering, known colloquially as Magic, or MtG, was created by Richard Garfield in 1993. It was the first ever trading card game and boasts over 20 million players worldwide.

The Gathering hosts weekly events on Monday nights, from 6:30PM to 11:30PM in the Yellow Room in MTCC, as well as draft and sealed events that require no previous collection! Come check us out whether you're an old pro, a beginner, or just want to see what the game's all about!
